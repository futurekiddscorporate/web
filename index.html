Our Programs

We meet kids at their level
regardless of their age

Playgroup & Daycare

(2-5 years)

Our Playgroup program aims at developing the essential foundational skills in your child, such as basic mannerisms and social and language development.

Nursery

(2-5 years)

Our Nursery program builds on the foundational skills and introduces structured learning to your child, which includes early literacy and numeracy

L.K.G

(4-10 years)

Our LKG program prepares your child for formal schooling with a more integrated preschool curriculum focusing on language and cognitive skills.

U.K.G

(5-12 years)

Our UKG program refines your child’s academic, social, and creative abilities and ensures that your child is perfectly ready for formal schooling
